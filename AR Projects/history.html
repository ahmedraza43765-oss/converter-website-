📜 History of Measurement Units

How humanity invented and standardized measurement

3000+ BC

The Cubit & Body-Based Units

The Egyptian cubit (~52 cm) was one of the first standardized units.

1790 — France

Birth of the Metric System

The meter defined as one ten-millionth of the equator-to-pole distance.

1875

Treaty of the Meter

17 nations signed the treaty, establishing global standards.

1960

The SI System

7 base units: meter, kilogram, second, ampere, kelvin, mole, candela.

1983

Speed of Light Definition

The meter redefined as distance light travels in 1/299,792,458 of a second.
