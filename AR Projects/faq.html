❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about measurements

How do I enter Scientific Notation?
Use 'e' notation: 3.5e6 means 3.5 × 10⁶.
Inches to millimeters?
1 inch = 25.4 mm exactly. Multiply by 25.4.
When are °C and °F the same?
At −40 degrees: −40°C = −40°F.
Why doesn't the US use metric?
Legally metric since 1866, but never fully adopted due to costs and resistance.
